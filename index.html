About the Conference

The conference on Algebraic Geometry will be held at Sun Yat-sen University, Guangzhou. It aims to bring together researchers and students to share recent progress in algebraic geometry and related fields.

All participants are welcome to attend the talks, exchange ideas, and discuss future collaborations.
